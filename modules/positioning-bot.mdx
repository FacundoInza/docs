## Three Positioning Modes

Choose the strategy that fits your operation. You can switch modes at any time without deactivating the bot.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Conservative">
    **Conservative mode** keeps your ad competitive without undercutting aggressively. The bot adjusts your price to stay near the top of the listings while respecting your margin floor — it won't race to the bottom just to be first.

    **Best for:** Merchants who prioritize margin stability, operations with consistent daily volume, and traders who want to stay competitive without constant price wars.

    **How it works:** The bot monitors the top listings and positions your ad within a defined range of the leading price. If the market shifts, your price follows — but only to the extent needed to maintain a strong position, not necessarily rank #1.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Aggressive">
    **Aggressive mode** always positions your ad at rank #1. The bot updates your price continuously to ensure you are always the first result buyers and sellers see.

    **Best for:** High-volume strategies where order flow is the top priority, operations during peak trading hours, and merchants who compete primarily on price.

    **How it works:** The bot tracks the current #1 listing and undercuts it by the minimum amount required to take the top spot. Prices update as fast as every 5 seconds to hold that position as competitors react.

    <Warning>
      Aggressive mode maximizes volume but applies the most price pressure on your margins. Always set a minimum price floor to protect your operation.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Fixed Price">
    **Fixed Price mode** holds your ad at a specific price you define. The bot does not adjust based on competitors — it simply maintains your chosen price and re-publishes the ad if it drifts.

    **Best for:** Fixed-margin strategies, operations where price consistency matters more than rank, and situations where you need predictable costs per trade.

    **How it works:** You set a target price. The bot monitors your ad and corrects any price drift, keeping the listing exactly where you want it.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How to Configure the Bot

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Positioning Bot module">
    In your Silver5 AI dashboard, navigate to **Modules → Positioning Bot**. You'll see your existing bots (if any) and a button to create a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your trading pair and account">
    Choose the trading pair you want to target (for example, **USDT/ARS**) and select the Binance account the bot should manage. Each bot is tied to one pair and one account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a positioning mode">
    Select **Conservative**, **Aggressive**, or **Fixed Price** based on your strategy. You can change this at any time after activation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your price floor and ceiling">
    Define a **minimum price floor** — the lowest price the bot is ever allowed to set — and a **maximum price ceiling**. These bounds protect your margin regardless of what the competition does.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure active hours (optional)">
    Set the time windows during which the bot should run. Outside those hours, the bot pauses automatically and your ad is left at its last price. This is useful if you only want to be competitive during business hours or peak trading windows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate the bot">
    Click **Activate**. The bot starts repricing immediately. You'll see the first price update within 5 seconds if market conditions require a change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Running Multiple Bots

You don't have to pick one strategy for your entire operation. Each Silver5 AI plan includes multiple bot slots — from 3 bots on the entry plan up to unlimited on Enterprise — so you can run separate configurations in parallel.

Common setups include:
- **One bot per trading pair** (e.g., USDT/ARS on one bot, USDT/BRL on another)
- **One bot per Binance account** if you operate multiple accounts
- **Mixed modes** — a Conservative bot running 24/7 and an Aggressive bot that you activate manually during peak hours

## Monitoring Your Bot

The Positioning Bot dashboard gives you full visibility into what the bot is doing in real time:

- **Live price feed** — see each price update as it happens, including the timestamp and the competitor price that triggered it
- **Current market rank** — your ad's position in the Binance P2P listings at this moment
- **Performance history** — a log of all price changes, your average rank over time, and order volume correlated with positioning events

<Tip>
  Start with **Conservative mode** to learn how your specific market moves and what price levels drive order flow. Once you understand the pattern, switch to **Aggressive** during your peak trading hours to capture maximum volume.
</Tip>
