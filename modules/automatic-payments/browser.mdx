The **Browser** variant runs your automatic payments **in the cloud**: you don't need to install any app or connect a phone. You configure the banking integration in Silver5 once and, from then on, every P2P order is processed automatically.

<Note>
  Does your bank require you to operate from the bank's mobile app? Use the [Mobile Automatic Payments](/modules/automatic-payments/mobile) variant, which operates the bank app on an Android phone from the Silver5 desktop app.
</Note>

## What it automates

When a P2P order comes in, Silver5 processes the payment in the cloud according to the banking integration you configured, without you having to switch tabs or confirm each operation by hand. Your operation runs end to end from the Silver5 dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the banking integration">
    In Silver5 → **Banking integrations → New**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the bank and the rail">
    Select the **bank** and the execution **rail**:

    - **Pago Móvil** — phone \+ ID number \+ destination bank.
    - **Interbank** — 20-digit account \+ ID number \+ destination bank.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link the integration to your ads">
    Link the integration to your Binance **ads** so that incoming orders use this payment flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test with a small real order">
    Before going live, verify the full flow with a low-amount real order.
  </Step>
</Steps>
