Centralized Chats consolidates all P2P conversations from every linked Binance account into a single inbox. Your team can respond, assign, and escalate messages without ever accessing Binance directly — keeping your credentials secure and your operation fully auditable.

## How to use Centralized Chats

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your Binance accounts">
    Link your Binance accounts to Silver5 via API key. See the [Connect Binance setup guide](#) for step-by-step instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your team members as operators">
    Go to **Settings → Team** and invite each operator by email. Assign roles and set access permissions at this step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Centralized Chats">
    Navigate to **Modules → Chats** and activate the module for each connected account. New P2P chats from all accounts flow into the shared inbox automatically from this point forward — operators see them as they arrive and respond directly from the Silver5 dashboard without ever accessing Binance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Chat assignment

Chats are assigned automatically based on each operator's current workload, so no single person gets overwhelmed while others sit idle. If you need to shift a conversation to a specific operator — for example, to a senior agent for a difficult case — you can reassign it manually from the inbox view in a single click.

## Alerts and keywords

Set up keyword alerts to flag urgent conversations for immediate human attention. Add terms like `"dispute"`, `"problem"`, `"refund"`, or `"police"` to your alert list, and Silver5 will surface those chats at the top of the queue and send a notification to the assigned operator. This keeps critical situations from getting buried in a busy inbox.

## Combined with AI Agent

Enable the AI Agent module to handle routine, repetitive messages automatically — greetings, payment confirmations, standard instructions — and only escalate complex or flagged chats to your human operators. This lets a small team manage a high volume of conversations without sacrificing quality. See the [AI Agent module](#) for setup details.

<Warning>
  If you remove an operator from your team, reassign their open chats first. Removing an operator with active conversations will leave those chats unattended until manually reassigned.
</Warning>
