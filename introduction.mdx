## What Silver5 AI automates

From the moment a customer sees your ad to the moment the order is released, Silver5 AI handles every step of your P2P operation:

- **Positioning Bot** — Reprices your ads every 5 seconds in conservative, aggressive, or fixed-price mode to keep you at the top of the Binance P2P market.
- **Order Management** — Captures and tracks every incoming order across all your connected Binance accounts in real time, with intelligent prioritization and a full lifecycle history.
- **Centralized Chats** — Brings all P2P conversations from multiple Binance accounts into a single unified inbox so your team can respond without switching tabs or sharing passwords.
- **AI Agent** — Responds to customers instantly in the Binance chat, guides them through the transaction, and triggers KYC verification automatically — no human needed for routine interactions.
- **KYC Verification** — Verifies customer identity via Didit or Sumsub in under 2 minutes, with biometric checks, liveness detection, AML screening, and document validation across 220+ countries.
- **Auto Messages** — Sends the right pre-configured message the moment a new order is created, a payment is received, or an order is completed — with zero manual effort.
- **Notifications** — Delivers real-time alerts about orders, payments, and KYC events via email and WhatsApp, using dynamic templates.
- **Banking Integration** — Connects your P2P operation to banking providers so payments can be verified and processed automatically on both buy and sell sides, with multi-currency support.

## Security

<Note>
  Silver5 AI never requests withdrawal permissions from your Binance account. Your API key is connected with read and P2P permissions only — your funds remain in your Binance account at all times, and Silver5 AI cannot move or custody them.
</Note>
