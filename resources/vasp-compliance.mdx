Virtual Asset Service Providers (VASPs) operating P2P desks face regulatory expectations around customer due diligence, transaction monitoring, and audit-ready record-keeping. Silver5 AI is purpose-built for this environment — automating the compliance touchpoints without slowing down your operation.

## Compliance features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Automated KYC" icon="id-card-clip">
    Identity verification via Didit or Sumsub with document scanning, biometric matching, and liveness detection. Verifications are triggered automatically by the AI Agent on new orders and complete in under 2 minutes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AML Screening" icon="shield-halved">
    Automatic screening of counterparties against international sanctions lists, politically exposed persons (PEP) databases, and adverse media sources — without manual lookups or separate tools.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audit Trail" icon="timeline">
    Every operator action, order event, and verification result is logged with a precise timestamp. The audit trail is immutable and available for review at any time — no manual logging required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Exportable Records" icon="file-arrow-down">
    Full client records and complete order history are available for export on demand. Use exports for internal compliance reviews, external audits, or regulatory reporting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multiple KYC Levels" icon="layer-group">
    Apply different verification requirements based on order size or customer risk classification. Configure up to 5 KYC levels on the Advanced plan, or unlimited levels on Enterprise — matching your compliance policy to transaction risk.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Secure Operator Access Model" icon="lock">
    Operators never access Binance directly. All actions are performed through Silver5's access layer, tied to individual operator accounts. You always know who approved, rejected, or interacted with any order or client record.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Silver5 AI is an operational automation platform, not a licensed compliance service or legal advisor. We provide tools to help you implement your compliance policies — you remain responsible for meeting your local regulatory requirements. Consult qualified legal and compliance advisors for guidance on your specific obligations.
</Warning>
