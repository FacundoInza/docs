## What's always included

No matter which plan you choose, every Silver5 AI account includes:

- **Unlimited orders** — process as many P2P buy and sell orders as your volume demands, with no per-order charges
- **Unlimited KYC verifications** — run identity checks on every counterparty without hitting a cap
- **All platform modules** — access to the full Silver5 AI feature set, including the centralized chat panel, order management, positioning bots, and notification system
- **No long-term contracts** — all plans are month-to-month; cancel or change at any time
- **7-day free trial** — try Silver5 AI in full for seven days with no credit card and no automatic charges
