Silver5 AI covers the full lifecycle of a Binance P2P operation — from keeping your ads competitive at the top of the market, all the way through order capture, customer chat, identity verification, and centralized team management. Every step connects to the next through the official Binance P2P API, with no credential sharing, no manual hand-offs, and a complete audit trail for every transaction.

## The 5-step automation flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Positioning Bot — stay first in the market">
    The Positioning Bot monitors your competitors' prices and automatically updates your ads every **5 seconds** to keep you at the top of the Binance P2P order book. Choose from three strategies — **conservative** (competitive without undercutting), **aggressive** (always first), or **fixed price** (hold a specific margin). Your ads stay visible and attractive around the clock without any manual price adjustments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order Capture — never miss an order">
    The moment a customer initiates a trade with you, Silver5 AI captures the order automatically across all your connected Binance accounts. Every order lands in your centralized dashboard with real-time status updates, customer data pulled via the API, and a full lifecycle history. Intelligent prioritization surfaces the orders that need attention first, so nothing slips through.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Agent — instant response and KYC request">
    Within seconds of a new order, your AI Agent sends a personalized greeting in the Binance chat and begins guiding the customer through the transaction. If your compliance flow requires identity verification, the agent requests it automatically — either by sending a Didit verification link or by collecting documents, selfies, and proof of life directly inside the Binance chat (Manual KYC mode). Your team only intervenes for complex edge cases.
  </Step>
  <Step title="KYC Verification — verified in under 2 minutes">
    Silver5 AI integrates with **Didit** and **Sumsub** to verify customer identity automatically. The process validates the customer's document (220+ countries supported), runs biometric liveness detection, checks against AML screening lists, and registers the result in your customer database — all in under 2 minutes. Verified status is reflected immediately in the order and chat view so your operator can release with confidence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Centralized Management — one dashboard for everything">
    Your entire operation — chats from all Binance accounts, order statuses, customer KYC records, payment notifications, and audit logs — lives in a single Silver5 AI dashboard. Multiple operators can work simultaneously without sharing Binance passwords. Every action is logged and exportable for compliance audits, giving you full visibility and control at any scale.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Platform architecture

Silver5 AI is built to run safely at scale without compromising your account security or operational independence:

- **Official Binance P2P API** — Silver5 AI connects exclusively through Binance's certified P2P API. No screen-scraping, no unofficial methods.
- **No credential sharing** — Operators access Silver5 AI's dashboard directly. No one needs your Binance username or password.
- **Read and P2P permissions only** — Your API key never grants withdrawal access. Your funds stay in your Binance account.
- **Full audit trail** — Every order, chat, KYC result, and system action is logged and exportable for compliance reviews.
- **Multi-account and multi-operator** — Connect multiple Binance accounts and assign multiple operators to the same workspace, each with their own access level.

The Positioning Bot fills your pipeline with orders; the AI Agent and KYC module handle each customer end-to-end; Centralized Chats and Notifications keep your team coordinated. Activating modules in isolation works — but the compounding effect of the full platform is where the real gains show up.
</Tip>
