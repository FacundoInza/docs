High-volume P2P operations face a compliance challenge: verifying identity manually for every counterparty creates friction, errors, and inconsistency. This use case shows how Silver5 AI automates the KYC process end to end — from initial verification to audit-ready records.

## The challenge

Manual KYC creates inconsistent records, delays, and relies on individual operator judgment. When volume is high, screenshots and spreadsheets don't scale — and regulators expect standardized, auditable evidence. One operator following up on a document via chat while another uses a different checklist means your compliance posture is only as strong as your least consistent team member.

## How Silver5 AI solves it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable KYC & Client Management and connect your verification provider">
    Activate the KYC & Client Management module and connect Didit (free) or Sumsub. Didit is available at no additional cost and integrates directly into the Silver5 workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the AI Agent to request KYC automatically on new orders">
    Configure the AI Agent to trigger a KYC request as soon as a new order is created. The customer receives a verification prompt within seconds — before any payment step begins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your KYC delivery mode">
    Select Link mode to redirect the customer to a hosted Didit verification page, or Manual mode to guide them through document submission directly inside the Binance P2P chat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The AI Agent guides the customer through document submission">
    The agent handles the entire customer-facing interaction — explaining what's needed, answering common questions, and following up if a submission is incomplete or unclear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Didit verifies the identity and returns a result">
    Document authenticity, biometric matching, and liveness detection are processed automatically. Results are returned in under 2 minutes in most cases.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Operators review and approve or reject from the Clients panel">
    Your team sees each verification result in the Clients panel alongside the linked order and chat history. Approval or rejection is a single action with a full record attached.
  </Step>
  <Step title="All records are stored with timestamps and linked to order history">
    Every verification — successful or failed — is logged with a timestamp and tied to the specific order and customer. Records are available for export at any time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
