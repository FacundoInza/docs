For high-volume ARS and BRL P2P operations, manually refreshing prices every few minutes during peak hours is unsustainable. This use case shows how the Positioning Bot and Order Management module work together to keep you competitive — while your operators focus on exceptions, not repetition.

## The challenge

During peak hours, competition shifts fast. A manual repricing delay of even a few minutes means losing orders to competitors. Meanwhile, order management without prioritization means important orders wait while low-value ones are handled first. The result is a predictable pattern: you're always reacting instead of leading, and your operators are buried in routine tasks that software should be handling for them.

## How Silver5 AI solves it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure the Positioning Bot with price floors and ceilings">
    Set explicit minimum and maximum price boundaries for your ARS and BRL pairs. The bot will only reprice within this range, protecting your margins automatically — no manual guardrails needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Aggressive or Conservative mode">
    Select the repricing mode that matches your spread strategy. Aggressive mode keeps you at the top of the listing even in fast-moving markets. Conservative mode preserves your margin during low-competition windows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Order Management to capture all orders across accounts">
    Order Management pulls in every new order from every connected Binance account automatically. No tab-switching, no missed notifications — every order appears in a single, unified view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure order prioritization">
    Set your prioritization rules by order age, value, and risk level. High-value or time-sensitive orders surface to the top of the queue so your operators always know what needs attention first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set alerts for orders that need human review">
    Define the conditions that trigger an operator alert — unusual payment methods, large amounts, or orders that exceed a configurable risk threshold. Everything else is handled automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
